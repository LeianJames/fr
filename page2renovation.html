Library Help & FAQs

You can search for books using the search bar at the top of the page. Enter keywords related to the book title. The search will look through all available books in our collection. Alternatively, there is also a searchbar inside each book sections to provide more accuracy.

Our library is organized into several categories:
  • Academics - Mathematics, Science, and other academic subjects
  • Financials - Books about finance and economics
  • Technology - Books about computers and digital technology
  • Fiction - Novels and stories
  • Thesis - Research papers and academic works
  • Religion - Books about faith and spirituality
  • Self Growth - Personal development books
  • Criminology - Books about law enforcement and criminal justice

When you find a book you're interested in, click the "Check Availability" button. This will show you whether the book is currently available for borrowing and its location in the library.

To borrow a book:
  1. Find the book you want in our catalog
  2. Check its availability
  3. Visit the library during operating hours
  4. Present your student ID at the circulation desk
  5. A librarian will assist you with the borrowing process

The library is open:
  • Monday to Saturday: 7:30 AM - 4:30 PM
  • Closed on Sundays and holidays

You can contact the library through:
